# Modak — Instagram Content Playbook (Brand Page)

## Voice & Tone
- **Warm, not preachy.** Like a wise friend who also has a toddler.
- **Emotionally honest.** We know parenting is hard. We never fake perfection.
- **Culturally rooted but accessible.** No gatekeeping. No Sanskrit tests.
- **Believable proof over promises.** Show real kids, real reactions, real moments.

A parent looks directly at camera and admits something vulnerable. No product mention for the first half. The reveal that Modak is the answer lands harder because the confession felt real.

```

(beat)
"But if you asked me to tell him the full Ramayana right now..."

[0:10-0:17] (Child turns to parent)
```

## Instagram Live Format: "Ask a Storyteller"

### Concept
Monthly or bi-weekly live session with a Modak content writer, voice artist, or cultural advisor. Parents ask questions live. Humanizes the brand and builds trust.

### Format
```
DURATION: 25-30 minutes
HOST: Modak community manager or content lead
GUEST: Voice narrator / cultural storyteller / child psychologist

SEGMENT 1 (5 min): Welcome + introduce guest
SEGMENT 2 (10 min): Guest shares how one story was researched, written, and recorded. Behind-the-scenes of a Modak story.
SEGMENT 3 (10 min): Live Q&A from Story questions. Parents ask:
- "How do you keep scary stories from frightening young kids?"
- "Which story is best for teaching honesty?"
- "Can I download stories for car rides?"
SEGMENT 4 (5 min): Exclusive reveal — upcoming story title + illustration preview. CTA to download app.

CTA: "This story drops in Modak next Thursday. Turn on app notifications to be first."
```

### Promotional Posts (Before Live)
```
STATIC POST (3 days before):
"Ask our storyteller anything this Thursday at 8 PM. Drop your questions below 👇"

STORY COUNTDOWN (24 hours before):
Countdown sticker: "Ask a Storyteller LIVE"
Text: "Pediatric Psychologist Dr. Nadia joins us to discuss: How do moral stories actually shape behavior?"

STORY REMINDER (1 hour before):
"Going live in 1 hour. Bring your hardest parenting question. We brought a professional."
```
