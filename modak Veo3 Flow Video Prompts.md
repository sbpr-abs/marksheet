## Prompt Engineering Notes for Veo 3 / Flow

**Structure every prompt with:**
1. **Subject** — who/what is in frame
2. **Action** — what is happening
3. **Environment** — where, lighting, time of day
4. **Mood / Tone** — emotional quality
5. **Camera** — movement, angle, distance
6. **Style** — cinematic, documentary, warm, etc.
7. **Negative space** — what to avoid

**Rules for 8-second clips:**
- One focused action per clip. No scene changes mid-clip.
- Camera movement must be slow and purposeful. Fast cuts confuse video models.
- Lighting description is critical. Specify warm/cool, source, and intensity.
- Include "smooth motion" and "natural movement" to avoid choppy output.
- Add "no text, no logos, no typography" — add text in post-production.

## CLIP 01: The Hand-Off (Bedtime Transition)
**Campaign:** Bedtime Was War / 20 Minutes to Breathe
**Use:** Hook in Reels, transition moment in 30s ads

```
A mother’s hands gently place a tablet into her child’s small hands.
The living room is dimly lit by a single warm amber table lamp.
The child, wearing soft pajamas, lies curled on a beige couch cushion.
As the tablet screen glows softly, the child’s face relaxes from mild tension
into calm curiosity. The mother’s hand lingers for a moment, then withdraws
slowly out of frame. Golden-hour quality light spilling from the lamp.
Soft focus background. Documentary-style intimacy. Slow-motion feel.
Smooth, natural hand movements. No abrupt motion. Warm, safe, serene mood.
No text. No logos. No cartoon characters visible. Cinematic shallow depth of field.
```

## Production Workflow for Veo 3 / Flow

### Step 1: Generate Each Clip
- Input prompt directly into Veo 3 or Flow.
- Request 8-second duration.
- Generate 3–5 variations per prompt. Pick the best.

### Step 2: Quality Check
- Motion smoothness? (Reject if choppy or wobbly.)
- Hand/finger coherence? (Reject if hands melt or glitch.)
- Lighting consistency? (Reject if light source jumps.)
- No text appearing? (Critical — add in post.)

### Step 3: Post-Production Additions
- Color grade: Warm gold family (add in editing, not prompt)
- Text overlays: Added in Premiere / CapCut / After Effects
- Modak logo: Added in final 2 seconds only
- Music / narration: Layered under or over, not dependent on generated audio

### Step 4: Format for Platform
| Platform | Aspect | Framerate | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Instagram Reels | 9:16 | 30fps | Text safe zone: center 70% |
| TikTok | 9:16 | 30fps | Hook in first 1.5 seconds |
| YouTube Shorts | 9:16 | 30fps | Keep end card 2 seconds |
| Facebook | 9:16 + 16:9 | 30fps | Generate horizontal cuts where possible |
| Stories | 9:16 | 30fps | Sticker overlay space at top |
