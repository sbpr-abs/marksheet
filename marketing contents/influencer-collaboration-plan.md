## Campaign Strategy

### Phase 1: Nano / Micro Pilot (Weeks 1–3)

**Goal:** Validate content angle + measure CPI

1. **Select 15 Nano + 10 Micro influencers** from Chennai/Bangalore (geo-targeted = lower CPI).
2. **Offer:** Free 1-year GD Math subscription + ₹5,000 per Reel for Micro / barter for Nano.
3. **Content brief:**
   - 7-day math challenge with their child
   - Before/after screen recording
   - Caption: "My 4-year-old learned counting in 1 week with this app"
4. **Tracking:** Unique UTM per influencer (`utm_source=insta&utm_medium=reel&utm_campaign=phase1&utm_content=<handle>`).
5. **Measure:** Track installs, cost per install, engagement rate.
6. **Kill / scale rule:** If CPI > ₹200, pause and revise content angle. If CPI < ₹80, expand.

### Phase 2: Micro / Mid Scale (Weeks 4–6)

**Goal:** Scale what worked in Phase 1

1. **Select 25 Micro + 10 Mid-tier influencers** across India.
2. **Offer:**
   - Micro: ₹15,000 – ₹25,000 per Reel + affiliate bonus
   - Mid: ₹40,000 – ₹70,000 per Reel + affiliate bonus
3. **Content brief:**
   - Dedicated 60-second Reel: "Why I chose this math app for my kid"
   - Stories series (3 slides) with swipe-up link
   - Comparison with other learning apps (objective, not scripted)
4. **Tracking:** UTM links + unique promo codes (`GD<HANDLE>` for 10 % off).
5. **KPIs:** 500+ installs, CPA < ₹300, engagement > 3 %.

### Phase 3: Macro / Mega Splash (Weeks 7–9)

**Goal:** Brand credibility + mass awareness

1. **Select 3 Macro + 1 Mega influencer** (parenting / education only).
2. **Offer:**
   - Macro: ₹1.5L – ₹2.5L for a 3-post Instagram package
   - Mega: ₹5L – ₹10L for 1 dedicated YouTube Short + 2 Instagram posts
3. **Content brief:**
   - Authentic integration: parent reviews their child's progress over 2 weeks
   - Link in bio for 72 hours
   - Story Q&A: "What app do you use for your kid's math?"
4. **Tracking:** UTM + promo code.
5. **KPIs:** Reach > 2M, installs > 1,000, brand search uplift.

### Phase 4: Optimize & Repeat (Weeks 10–12)

1. **Analyze:** Which tiers drove best CPA? Which content angle converted?
2. **Double down:** Re-book top 5 performers from Phase 1–2 as brand ambassadors (3-month retainer).
3. **Drop:** Influencers with CPI > ₹400 or no installs.
4. **Plan Q2:** Shift 70 % budget to best-performing tier.

## Tracking & UTM Setup

### UTM Link Structure

```
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=in.abstractit.gd.math
  &referrer=utm_source%3D<platform>%26utm_medium%3D<post_type>%26
  utm_campaign%3D<phase>%26utm_content%3D<handle>
```

### Examples

| Influencer | UTM Link |
|---|---|
| `@nilan_and_mommy` | `...&referrer=utm_source%3DInstagram%26utm_medium%3Dreel%26utm_campaign%3Dphase2%26utm_content%3Dnilan_and_mommy` |
| `@vinitha_parthiban` | `...&referrer=utm_source%3DInstagram%26utm_medium%3Dreel%26utm_campaign%3Dphase1%26utm_content%3Dvinitha_parthiban` |
| CarryMinati | `...&referrer=utm_source%3DYouTube%26utm_medium%3Dshort%26utm_campaign%3Dphase3%26utm_content%3Dcarryminati` |

### Promo Code Strategy

Assign each influencer a unique code: `GD<HANDLE>` (e.g., `GDNILAN`, `GDVINITHA`).

- 10 % discount for users
- Tracks conversions back to influencer
- Bonus paid to influencer per conversion
