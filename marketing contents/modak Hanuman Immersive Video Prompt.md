# Modak — "Jai Hanuman" Immersive Story Ad
## 40-Second Vertical Cinematic Video (9:16) — Veo 3 / Flow Prompts

---

## Ad Concept (Elaborated)

A 7-year-old Indian boy discovers the Hanuman story inside Modak and literally steps into the myth. The camera treats his imagination as real — no cartoon filters, no over-the-top fantasy VFX. Instead: cinematic golden light, wind, dust, and a child who believes so fully that the world reshapes around him.

**Core emotional arc:**
1. **Curiosity** (the tap)
2. **Awe** (the doorway opens)
3. **Courage** (the leap — mid-air, real wind, real doubt)
4. **Triumph** (the mace, the roar, the resolve)
5. **Return** (back to the room, changed)
6. **CTA** (the app revealed)

**Tone:** Live-action mythological realism. Think *RRR* meets *The Secret of Roan Inish* — a child’s imagination rendered with the weight of real cinema.

### CLIP 01 — "The Discovery" (0:00–0:08)
**Role:** Hook. The tap that opens the world.

**Story:**
Vihaan is curled on a moss-green couch in a warm Indian living room at dusk. He unlocks a tablet. Modak’s home screen glows softly. His thumb hovers, then taps the Hanuman story card. His face changes — he leans in, eyebrows rising, a silent "yes" forming on his lips.

**Veo 3 / Flow Prompt:**
```
An over-the-shoulder shot of a 7-year-old Indian boy sitting cross-legged
on a moss-green woven couch in a warm living room at golden hour.
He holds a tablet in both hands. His face is lit by soft amber screen glow.
On the tablet: a warm UI showing a story card with a majestic orange-and-gold
Hanuman illustration. His thumb moves and taps the card.
As he taps, his eyes widen slightly and his mouth opens in quiet excitement.
Camera is low, behind his shoulder, intimate. Warm lamplight in foreground,
fading blue twilight through a tall window in the background.
Dust motes drift through golden light. Living room details: wooden bookshelf,
textured rug, ceramic cups on a low table. Documentary realism.
Smooth motion. Natural child hand movement. No abrupt motion.
Shallow depth of field. Cinematic 9:16 vertical framing.
No text. No logos on screen. No UI text readable.
Warm, gold-drenched, sacred-ordinary mood.
```

**Transition out:** Hard cut to black — then golden light bleeds in.

---

### CLIP 02 — "The Door Opens" (0:08–0:16)
**Role:** The bridge. Reality cracks open.

**Story:**
Vihaan looks up from the tablet. Over his shoulder, a shaft of warm light appears on the far wall of the living room — not from any lamp. He turns his head. The light pulses once, like a breathing doorway.

**Veo 3 / Flow Prompt:**
```
Close-up on a 7-year-old Indian boy’s face, golden-brown skin, messy black hair,
sage-green t-shirt. He has just looked up from a glowing tablet.
His eyes are wide, reflected golden light dancing in his pupils.
His expression shifts from reading-focus to stunned curiosity.
Behind him, out of focus, a vertical shaft of warm saffron-orange light
appears on a textured white wall — impossible, sourceless, pulsing gently.
The living room fades into shadow except for this light.
Camera holds on his face. Very slow push-in, almost imperceptible.
Cinematic shallow depth of field. Dust floating through the amber shaft.
Golden-hour quality but supernatural. Reverent, mysterious, not scary.
Smooth continuous motion. No fast camera moves.
Film grain texture. Warm undertones.
No text. No logos. Intimate documentary realism.
```

**Transition out:** Match-cut — the light flares to white, dissolving into wind.

### CLIP 03 — "The Leap" (0:16–0:24)
**Role:** Action core. He crosses into the story.

**Story:**
Vihaan stands at the edge of a vast landscape. He is still in his sage-green t-shirt and shorts — no costume change. A warm wind lifts his hair. He looks small against enormous sky. He takes a running start and leaps — not flying like a superhero, but suspended in mid-air, arms spread, face lit by triumph and terror.

**Veo 3 / Flow Prompt:**
```
Wide shot of a small 7-year-old Indian boy standing at the edge
of a vast golden-brown cliff. He wears the same sage-green t-shirt
and navy shorts from the living room — real clothes, no costume.
Behind him: an enormous sky filled with rolling saffron and indigo clouds,
like a Rajasthani sunset made epic. Wind lifts his hair and t-shirt hem.
He looks tiny against the scale. The ground is dry red earth and pale grass.
He takes three running steps toward the edge and leaps.
Camera follows him in slow motion as he launches into the air,
arms spread wide, face turned upward, expression a mix of exhilaration
and disbelief — not a superhero, just a brave child who believed.
Golden light bursts from behind the clouds as he jumps.
Cinematic wide 9:16 vertical. Low angle, making the height feel real.
Wind particles, dust, grass flying. No costume change. No mace yet.
Smooth, continuous, slow-motion leap. Natural child body physics.
No text. No logos. Mythological landscape rendered as real geography.
```

**Transition out:** Hard cut mid-air to landing — continuity of motion maintained.

### CLIP 04 — "The Fight" (0:24–0:32)
**Role:** Climax. He becomes the story.

**Story:**
Vihaan lands in soft desert dust. Before him: shadowy figures (suggestive, not graphic — swirling dust masses). He grips a wooden rod that glows warm gold at its tip — not a CGI mace, a real branch that looks sacred in this light. He swings with all his strength. The dust scatters in the wind.

**Veo 3 / Flow Prompt:**
```
Ground-level shot of a 7-year-old Indian boy landing hard in desert dust,
sage-green t-shirt, navy shorts. Dust explodes around his bare feet.
He drops to one knee for balance, then rises.
Shadowy, swirling dust-figures advance toward him — obscured,
not monstrous, abstract opponents of wind and shadow.
He grabs a fallen wooden branch lying in the sand.
As his fingers close around it, the wood begins to glow warm gold
from within, like heated embers inside bark.
He swings with both hands, whole body rotating into the strike.
The branch cuts through the air. On contact, the shadow-mass disintegrates
into golden particles and wind. His face is fierce, eyes narrowed,
jaw set — real effort, not performative.
Camera tracks the swing in medium shot, then whip-pans to his reaction.
Cinematic action framing in 9:16 vertical. Fast but readable motion.
Warm amber and gold lighting. No blood. No scary faces.
Smooth, grounded child movement. Real physics. No flying.
No text. No logos. The myth rendered as real childhood courage.
```

**Transition out:** Freeze on his victorious expression — then golden particles pull back to reveal the room.

### CLIP 05 — "The Return" (0:32–0:38)
**Role:** Emotional resolution. He’s back. Changed.

**Story:**
Vihaan is back on the couch, exactly where he started. Tablet in his lap, the Hanuman story still glowing. But his posture is different — shoulders back, chin up, a small, knowing smile. A warm breeze (from a ceiling fan) ruffles his hair. He looks not entertained — but empowered.

**Veo 3 / Flow Prompt:**
```
Medium close-up of the same 7-year-old Indian boy, back on the moss-green couch.
Same living room. Same golden hour. Tablet still glowing softly in his lap.
But his posture has shifted: shoulders straight, chin slightly raised,
a small, confident smile — not a grin, a knowing half-smile.
Warm breeze from a ceiling fan gently moves his hair across his forehead.
His eyes hold the golden light from before, like he brought some back.
Camera slowly pulls back from a close-up to a medium shot,
revealing the cozy living room still intact: bookshelf, cups, rug.
The vertical saffron light on the wall is gone. Everything ordinary.
Everything changed. Documentary warmth. Soft, continuous camera retreat.
Cinematic 9:16 vertical. Warm gold and amber throughout.
No text. No logos. Sacred ordinary moment.
```

**Transition out:** Gentle fade to black over 1 second.

## Cinematic Direction Notes (Post-Production)

### Transitions
| Cut | Method | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Clip 1 → 2 | Hard cut to light bleed | Shock of the impossible |
| Clip 2 → 3 | Match-cut: light flare to wind | The doorway becomes the sky |
| Clip 3 → 4 | Action continuity: mid-air → landing | Seamless dream logic |
| Clip 4 → 5 | Gold particles dissolve to room | Magic returning to everyday life |
| Clip 5 → End | Gentle fade to black | Breath. Then the promise. |

### Color Grade
- **Clips 1, 2, 5:** Warm gold family, slightly desaturated greens, amber highlights.
- **Clips 3, 4:** Pushed warm — saffron, red earth, gold. No teal/orange. Never cold.
- **End card:** Near-monochrome. Gold on black. White text.

### Music / Sound Design
- **0:00–0:08:** Soft room tone. Fingers on tablet. A single warm tanpura drone begins.
- **0:08–0:16:** The drone swells. Wind enters. No melody yet — just tension.
- **0:16–0:24:** Percussion kicks in — not Bollywood, but traditional *dholak* and breathy flute. Roar of wind over cliff.
- **0:24–0:32:** Peak of the percussion. Impact sound on the swing. Wind scattering dust.
- **0:32–0:38:** All instruments drop out. Tanpura returns, warmer now. Room tone. A soft chime.
- **0:38–0:40:** Silence. Single warm tone under text.

### Text Overlay Rules
- **No text during clips.** The story must work without words.
- **End card only.** The words are: brand, promise, platform.
- **If subtitles are required for accessibility:** Add after generation in post. White serif text, bottom third, gentle fade in/out.
